Interviewer: What kind of work do you do?

  • Interviewee 1: I am a software engineer at Frostbite, a proprietary of Electronic Arts. I maintain and develop features for our proprietary game engine which enables game teams to develop games with ease and quality.
  • Interviewee 2: I develop software at Certsy, which is a part of Seek, writing software which facilitates individuals to verify their identity. Aiding companies in finding the right person for their jobs.
  • Interviewee 3: I develop software at Certsy, which is a part of Seek, writing software which facilitates individuals to verify their identity. Aiding companies in finding the right person for their jobs.
  • Interviewee 4: I am full stack engineer in Malaysia, the company I work for is a job board like seek, at first I was doing a lot of doc fixing, but now I’m getting work on some new stuff, I work on both the front and back end of some aspects of the company, mainly developing in C#.

Interviewer: What kind of people do you interact with? (other IT professionals, clients, investors, the general public)

  • Interviewee 1: I communicate with other IT professionals, which includes technical directors and other software engineers. Because I work for a proprietary developing company I also communicate with internal stakeholders.
  • Interviewee 2: I interact with other developers, other non-developers and operations, who are people who review our work.
  • Interviewee 3: I interact with other developers, other non-developers and operations, who are people who review our work.
  • Interviewee 4: I communicate with other IT professionals, I’m not in contact with clients generally.

Interviewer: Where do you spend most of your time?

  • Interviewee 1: I spend most of my time in front of my computer and in the office. Due to the current situation, I'm working from home though.
  • Interviewee 2: Currently in today’s climate and circumstances, I spend my time at home on the computer, but I would be spending most of my time at the office.
  • Interviewee 3: I spend most of my time at the office, however, due to the current circumstances, I currently spend most of time working from home.
  • Interviewee 4: I spend most of my time in the office, I sometimes work from home, however, because of the current situation I am working from home.

Interviewer: What aspect of your position do you find the most challenging?

  • Interviewee 1: Communicating with other members of frostbite due to the time difference. The development of the frostbite engine spans multiple time-zones, so getting a response can take a while.
  • Interviewee 2: Trying to communicate with other team members and also trying to understand their viewpoint.
  • Interviewee 3: I am the most junior in my role, I don’t really have experience in the front end, so trying to understand how everything works is pretty challenging.
  • Interviewee 4: Having to work on something or work on queries without having the information, I understand how everything works, but when things are allocated to another team, it makes it a bit difficult to know what’s what.
